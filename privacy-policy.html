Budget Daily (“the App”) is developed by Jonathan Larsen. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Budget Daily does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not collect:

  • Personal data
  • Financial account information
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers

All data remains on your device.

2. Local Device Storage

Your budgets, categories, expenses, incomes, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData system. We do not have access to this data.

3. Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. All storage and syncing is handled by Apple. We do not receive any of this data.

4. Payments

Budget Daily is a paid app. All payments are handled by Apple’s App Store. We do not process or store payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information and is safe for all ages. However, parents should supervise financial apps for minors.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising. The only external services are:

  • Apple (App Store and iCloud)
  • GitHub Pages (this policy)

7. Security

Your data is protected by Apple’s built-in security features. We never transmit your data to any server we control.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect data:

  • We cannot access your data
  • We cannot delete your data

All data deletion is done by removing the App or managing your iCloud settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available here.
